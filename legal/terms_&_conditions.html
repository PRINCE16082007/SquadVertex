1. Definitions

1.1 Creators

Users who host an event by paying the required seed fee.

1.2 Participants

Users who engage with or enter an event.

1.3 Seed

Total gross amount collected from creators (minimum 3 creators × ₹200 each = ₹600).

1.4 Grand Prize Pool (GP)

Sum of Seed plus all raw donations collected for an event.

1.5 Platform Profit

20% of GP, retained by SquadVertex.

1.6 Remaining Prize Pool

GP minus Platform Profit, distributed among winners.

1.7 Winners

Top three participants, ranked 1st, 2nd, and 3rd.

1.8 SVX Points

Internal, non-redeemable reward currency awarded according to placement; not convertible to cash or other value.

2. Event Participation & Fees

2.1 An event requires a minimum of three paying Creators to commence.

2.2 Each Creator must pay a minimum of ₹200 in cash (they may choose to pay more for additional prize funding); no SVX, vouchers, or partial payments accepted.

2.3 If fewer than three Creators pay:

  • Event is automatically canceled
  • Non-paying Creators receive a 50% refund of their seed (₹100) per non-payer

3. Donations

3.1 Donations are voluntary gifts with no contractual promise of goods or services.

3.2 All raw Donations collected (UPI, PayPal) are added in full to GP. No fees on donations.

3.3 Donations are not subject to GST when no service or product is promised in return.

4. Prize Pool Calculations

4.1 GP Calculation: GP = Seed + Raw Donations

4.2 Platform Profit: 20% of GP

4.3 Remaining Prize Pool: GP − Platform Profit

4.4 Winner Payouts:

  • 1st place: 50% of Remaining Prize Pool
  • 2nd place: 30% of Remaining Prize Pool
  • 3rd place: 20% of Remaining Prize Pool

4.5 All monetary payouts are in rupees (₹) and rounded to the nearest rupee for distribution.
