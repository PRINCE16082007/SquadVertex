Total Income (2025)

The total amount of money earned in the current year. This includes all revenue sources and transactions recorded in the system.

Top Revenue Source

The primary source contributing the most to your income. This helps identify which business area is most profitable.

Average Transaction Size

The average amount per transaction. Calculated by dividing total income by the number of transactions.

Monthly Growth Rate

The percentage increase in income compared to the previous month. Helps track business growth trends.

Total Invested

The total amount of money invested across all investment types in the current year.

Investment ROI

Return on Investment percentage. Measures the profitability of your investments relative to the cost.

Investment Growth Rate

The percentage increase in your investment portfolio value over time.

Investment Efficiency

A measure of how effectively your investments are generating returns. Higher percentage indicates better performance.
