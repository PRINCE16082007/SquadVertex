SquadVertex — Refund & Dispute Policy

Effective: August 19, 2025

TL;DR: Quick Summary

  • Only Events are eligible for cash refunds (under strict conditions).
  • Everything else (donations, subscriptions, digital assets, completed services, promotions, trials) is explicitly non-refundable.
  • Refunds are 50% of the amount SquadVertex actually received (after payment processor fees and GST).
  • Refunds are processed in 7–14 business days after approval.
  • Use the Refund Page for refund requests and appeals. Use the separate Dispute Page for all other issues.

Scope: This policy applies to all transactions on the SquadVertex website.

1. Key Event Rules (Must-Know)

Refunds apply only to Events that meet the conditions below.

  • Cancellation Deadline: Requests must be submitted at least 48 hours before the event’s scheduled start time.
  • 70% Creator Agreement: Creator-initiated cancellations require confirmation from 70% or more of the event's creators (collected via email by SquadVertex) to trigger refunds. Confirmations are valid up to 12 hours before event start.
  • Fees Not Received: If a 3-creator event fails to receive the required fees 24 hours before its start, SquadVertex may auto-cancel it and issue refunds per the 50% rule.

2. Eligible Cancellations & Refunds

Platform-Initiated Cancellation (<24-Hour Fees)

If a 3-creator event does not have its required fees collected 24 hours before start, SquadVertex will cancel it.

  • Paying creators receive a 50% cash refund of the net amount SquadVertex received.
  • Non-paying creators receive nothing.

Creator-Initiated Cancellation (70% Rule)

If creators agree (70%+) and request cancellation ≥48 hours before the event starts, paying creators receive a 50% cash refund of the net amount received.

If the 70% confirmation threshold is not met, no refund is issued, and SquadVertex retains all funds.

3. Refund Calculation & Examples

Refunds are calculated solely on the amount SquadVertex actually received from the payment processor, after deductions for GST and processor fees. SquadVertex does not reimburse these fees.

Scenario Amount User Paid Amount SV Received* Refund (50%)
Standard Event Cancellation $100.00 $90.00 $45.00
Postponement Manipulation (30%) $100.00 $90.00 $27.00

*Example amount. The actual net amount received varies by transaction.

4. The Postponement Loophole Rule (Anti-Gaming)

Creators may postpone events. However, to prevent manipulation of the cancellation window:

If a postponement is used to manipulate the policy (e.g., postponing to a later date to make a cancellation request appear to meet the 48-hour deadline), any subsequent cancellation will be treated as a reduced-refund case.

  • Reduced Refund: In such cases, eligible paying creators will receive a 30% cash refund (calculated on received funds) instead of 50%. All standard confirmation thresholds still apply.

5. Alternative: SVX Points (Goodwill)

At its sole discretion, SquadVertex may offer SVX points equal to 75% of the user’s paid amount (based on funds received) in eligible edge cases instead of a cash refund. SVX credits are for use on the platform only, are non-transferable, and are governed by the SVX Points terms.

6. Fraud & Security (EXELIS)

If our EXELIS security system detects credible fraud, bots, DDoS, manipulative behavior, or other red flags, SquadVertex may immediately terminate related accounts and block access.

  • In confirmed cases of fraud or cheating, no refunds will be issued under any circumstance, and SquadVertex retains all funds.
  • If a user believes their account was terminated in error, they may contact support to request a review at SquadVertex's discretion.

7. Technical Failures & Compensation

If a platform error materially affects an event, SquadVertex may offer compensation at its discretion.

  • Minor Bug: Affecting a specific user action typically results in support assistance only.
  • Event-Impacting Bug: Typically results in a 15% compensation of the event money (paid from the GP pool) to affected creators.
  • Severe Platform Fault: In extreme, demonstrable cases, SquadVertex may issue up to a 100% refund.

Strong proof is required. No refund or compensation is offered for issues not caused by the platform.

8. Explicitly Non-Refundable Items

The following are strictly non-refundable:

  • Donations (to creators or the platform)
  • Digital assets, downloads, or in-platform purchases
  • Subscriptions
  • Completed services
  • Promotional items and any offers without a trial

9. How to Request a Refund

All refund requests must be submitted through the official Refund Page.

Initial Review Form (Required Fields):

  • Full Name (as on your SquadVertex account)
  • SquadVertex Username / Creator ID
  • Account Email
  • Event Name & Event ID
  • Transaction ID(s) / Payment Reference
  • Amount Paid & Currency
  • Refund Reason (Event Cancellation / Technical Compensation / Other)
  • Date & Time of Event and Issue
  • Short Description of the Issue (max 250 words)
  • Attachments: Original payment proof, logs, timestamps, or video evidence. Screenshots alone may be insufficient.
  • Preferred Refund Method (e.g., PayPal email, UPI ID)
  • Declaration of accuracy and acceptance of policy terms.

Submission generates an auto-acknowledgement and a support ticket.

Re-Review (Appeal) Process:

If your initial request is denied, you may submit one Re-Review request on the same Refund Page. You must include:

  • All fields from the Initial Review form.
  • The ticket number from your initial request.
  • A short appeal statement (max 100 words) and any new evidence.

The decision after a Re-Review is final.

10. Dispute Process (For Non-Refund Issues)

For issues such as chargeback disputes, account disputes, or payout disagreements, you must use the separate Dispute Page. This follows a different ticket workflow and escalation process.

11. Processing Timeline & Communication

  • Review Time: All requests are reviewed by a human agent. Reviews are not instant.
  • Payout Processing: Approved refunds are processed within 7–14 business days. Delays by payment processors or banks are beyond our control.
  • Business Hours: 09:00 – 18:00 IST (GMT +5:30), Monday through Saturday. Sunday is not a business day.
  • Official Contact: squadvertex24official@gmail.com

12. Policy Integrity

This policy is designed to be fair and transparent for all users. It grants special consideration only to Events under specific conditions, while all other transactions are final. We encourage flexible scheduling but will strictly enforce rules against deadline manipulation and fraudulent activity. Our commitment is to maintain a secure and trustworthy platform. In cases of severe platform error, we will act to rectify the situation fairly.

SquadVertex may update this policy. Continued use of the site after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the new terms. Major changes will be indicated by a new Effective Date at the top of this page.
