Admission Module Instructions

  • Only admin/admission staff can access this page.
  • Student details (name, gender, DOB, parent's/guardian names, etc.) are entered and documents verified.
  • Optional PDF upload for additional documents is provided.
  • A unique SR number field is created (roll number remains empty until assigned later).
  • An automatic timestamp is recorded on submission.
  • Recent Admission Requests are fetched from a separate collection.
  • Recent Admissions are dynamically loaded from the students subcollection and support quick search.
  • All changes and data management in this module are the responsibility of the admin.

Note: All changes and updates to this module are managed solely by the admin.
