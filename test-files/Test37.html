Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains how SquadVertex collects, uses, and protects your information.

Effective Date: October 1, 2025 | Last Updated: October 1, 2025

1. Introduction

At SquadVertex, we value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our platform or participate in events.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Visitors (Voting Participants)

  • Email Address: To generate a unique Voting ID for reliable participation in polls
  • Voting Data: Details of your voting activity to ensure the integrity of results
  • Usage Data: Pages visited, session duration, device type, browser type

2.2 Members (Profile Creators)

  • Personal Information: Name, Email Address, Password, and YouTube Channel Link
  • Profile Details: Optional profile picture, bio, and uploaded media (e.g., logos, backgrounds)
  • Content Details: Replay files, videos, and other materials shared for feature use

2.3 Payment Information

  • Transaction Data: Transaction IDs, UPI details, PayPal email (payment processors store card/bank details)
  • Event Participation: Votes, likes, comments, timestamps, IP address

2.4 Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to remember preferences, improve functionality, and analyze usage. You may disable cookies via your browser, but some features may not function properly.

3. How We Use Your Information

3.1 For Visitors:

  • To generate and manage reliable Voting IDs
  • To prevent duplicate or fraudulent voting

3.2 For Members:

  • To create and manage your profile
  • To display your shared YouTube content on SquadVertex
  • To promote SquadVertex through your content (with your explicit consent)
  • To send updates, promotional offers, and platform notifications

3.3 General Usage:

  • Service Delivery: Process event participation, calculate prize pools
  • Payment Processing: Verify and record payments for seed fees, donations, subscriptions
  • Security: Detect and prevent fraud and abuse
  • Communication: Respond to inquiries, send confirmations and updates
  • Improvement & Analytics: Understand usage patterns, optimize user experience

4. Consent and Usage of Content

By signing up and providing your YouTube channel link, you grant SquadVertex the right to use your shared content for:

  • Showcasing your channel and videos on the platform
  • Promotional activities, such as banners, advertisements, or custom portals

Important: You retain full ownership of your content. If you wish to withdraw this consent or remove specific content, you can contact us or adjust your profile settings.

5. Data Protection

We prioritize the security of your data with measures including:

  • Encryption: All personal information is encrypted in transit and at rest
  • Access Control: Only authorized personnel can access sensitive data
  • Secure Storage: Data is stored on Firebase, which uses industry-standard security practices
  • Administrative Safeguards: Regular security audits and employee training

While we strive to protect your data, no transmission over the internet can be guaranteed 100% secure.

6. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell or share your data with third parties, except:

  • Service Providers: Trusted third parties who assist us in running SquadVertex, bound by confidentiality agreements (e.g., PayPal, UPI providers, email services)
  • Legal Obligations: When required by law or regulatory authorities
  • Business Transfers: If merging, acquiring, or selling assets, user data may be transferred under confidentiality terms

7. Data Retention

We retain personal data as long as necessary to:

  • Fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy
  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Resolve disputes and enforce agreements

You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time, subject to operational needs (e.g., pending payouts).

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: View the data we store about you
  • Correction: Update or correct inaccuracies in your profile
  • Deletion: Delete your account and all associated data
  • Opt-Out: Revoke consent for promotional use of your content and unsubscribe from marketing communications

9. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from minors. If you believe we have collected data from a minor, please contact us to remove it.

10. Copyright and Intellectual Property
