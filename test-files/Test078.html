Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about the event

How can I watch the event live?
The event will be streamed live on YouTube and Twitch. We'll send all registered attendees direct links to the streams 24 hours before the event starts. You can also watch from our website on the event day.
How does the voting work?
During the event, you'll be able to vote for your favorite creators through our real-time voting platform. Each registered attendee gets 5 votes to distribute however they choose. Voting will be open during specific segments of the event.
Are there any prizes for attendees?
Absolutely! Throughout the event, we'll be giving away exclusive merchandise, gift cards, and even some creator meet-and-greet opportunities. All attendees are automatically entered into our prize drawings just by being present during the live stream.
Will the event be recorded?
Yes, the entire event will be recorded and made available on our YouTube channel within 48 hours after the live broadcast ends. Highlights and special moments will be shared across our social media platforms.
Can I interact with the creators during the event?
Definitely! There will be dedicated Q&A sessions where you can submit questions that creators will answer live. Additionally, creators may interact with the chat during their segments, so make sure to participate actively!
