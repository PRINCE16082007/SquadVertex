What Is SquadVertex?

SquadVertex is your all-in-one creator basecamp. We give you the tools to launch a professional web presence—whether you’re an artist, writer, musician or maker. Instantly spin up a sleek site, curate an image gallery, sell your digital creations, and put on live streams. All optimized for speed and simplicity, with zero juggle of servers or CDN hassles.

Core Features

🌐

VertexSite

Ready-made template engine—no drag-drop clutter.

  • SquadLite: 1 fixed template
  • SquadPro: choose from 5 pro templates
  • SquadElite: access 15 premium templates
  • All templates are mobile-responsive & instantly deployable
🖼️

PixelVault

Image gallery & portfolio—showcase artwork, photography, designs or comics.

  • Organize by albums, add captions
  • Lightbox previews
  • Social share links
🛒

SquadStore

Sell digital assets—ebooks, graphics packs, music tracks, presets or Photoshop brushes.

  • Upload files, set your price
  • Share your storefront link
  • No video downloads—storage optimized for images & documents only

SquadStage

Unlimited live event hosting—request your slot anytime.

  • We handle the stream infra; you focus on content
  • Pay-per-event pricing keeps plans lean

Note: All plans include the same unlimited event requests—just pick your date, set your fee, and go live.

💬

SquadCircle

Community hub—your personal announcement board.

  • Post updates, polls, and link to your other channels
  • Members can comment, react, and share
