💬 Final Summary

The Wix Core Plan ($4.99/month) does support collections and offers 50 GB storage — making it viable for a basic static site or micro-community.

However, the 4,000-field database limit severely restricts growth and interactivity.

In contrast, the current platform delivers greater capacity, real-time functionality, 40× more storage, and room to scale — all at a comparable or lower cost.
