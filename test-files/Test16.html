Common questions

Can I upgrade my plan later? +
Yes, you can upgrade at any time. Your billing will be prorated based on your current plan.
How long does activation take? +
Manual review typically takes 24-72 hours. We'll notify you immediately when your account is ready.
Are events included in the pricing? +
Events are charged separately based on duration and attendance. SquadPro includes one 30-min event slot.
Can I pay in INR instead of USD? +
All prices are in USD, but our payment gateway will automatically convert to your local currency.
What's your refund policy? +
We offer a 14-day refund policy for annual plans. Monthly plans can be canceled anytime.
