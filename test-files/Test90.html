Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about event setup, payments, prizes, and more

Event Setup & Participation

Q1.1: How many creators are needed to start an event?

A: A minimum of three (3) creators must each pay at least ₹200 to seed the event.

Q1.2: Can creators pay more than ₹200?

A: Yes—each creator must pay a minimum of ₹200; higher contributions are welcome to increase the prize pool.

Q1.3: What happens if fewer than three creators pay?

A: The event is automatically canceled. Non-paying creators receive a 50% refund (₹100 per non-payer).

Q1.4: Can creators seed with SVX or vouchers?

A: No—only cash payments (UPI, PayPal) are accepted for seeding.

Donations & Fees

Q2.1: Are there fees on donations?

A: No—100% of raw donations go into the Grand Prize Pool (GP).

Q2.2: Is donation money taxable?

A: Donations are non-taxable if no goods or services are contractually promised in return.

Q2.3: How do I donate?

A: Use the provided UPI QR code or PayPal link; your contribution directly boosts the prize pool.

Prize Pool & Platform Profit

Q3.1: How is the Grand Prize Pool (GP) calculated?

A: GP = Total Seed (all creators' payments) + Total Donations.

Q3.2: What share does SquadVertex keep?

A: SquadVertex retains 20% of GP as platform profit.

Q3.3: How is the remaining prize money distributed?

A: Remaining 80% of GP is split: 1st=50%, 2nd=30%, 3rd=20%.

Q3.4: Can I see an example calculation?

A: Yes—if Seed=₹600 and Donations=₹1,000:

GP = ₹600 + ₹1,000 = ₹1,600

Platform Share = 20% of ₹1,600 = ₹320

Remaining Prize Pool = ₹1,280

1st Place = 50% of ₹1,280 = ₹640

2nd Place = 30% of ₹1,280 = ₹384

3rd Place = 20% of ₹1,280 = ₹256

SVX Points

Q4.1: What are SVX Points?

A: Internal reward points; non-redeemable for cash, used for in-platform perks.

Q4.2: How are SVX Points awarded to winners?

A: Based on GP bands (per ₹10K up to ₹50K); capped at 1,000 SVX for 1st place.

Grand Prize Pool SVX Points for 1st Place
GP ≤ ₹10K 500 SVX
₹10K–₹20K 625 SVX
₹20K–₹30K 750 SVX
₹30K–₹40K 875 SVX
₹40K–₹50K 1,000 SVX
GP > ₹50K 1,000 SVX
Q4.3: When do SVX Points arrive?

A: Within 24 hours after event closure and manual verification.

Subscriptions & Digital Assets

Q5.1: How do subscriptions work?

A: Subscriptions are purchased via PayPal; prices include all applicable GST and are displayed in USD/INR with SVX equivalents.

Q5.2: How are digital assets priced?

A: All digital assets are priced in INR plus GST. E.g., ₹100 base → displayed as ₹118 (incl. 18% GST).

Q5.3: How is the creator's share calculated on asset sales?

A: From the base price: SquadVertex retains 10%; the rest goes to the creator. GST is segregated separately.

Taxes & GST

Q6.1: Do I need to pay GST?

A: SquadVertex charges and remits GST on all taxable sales. You only pay GST on purchases if required by law.

Q6.2: When does SquadVertex register for GST?

A: We register once our annual turnover crosses the legal threshold (₹20 lakh). Until then, GST is not applied on our service fees.

Timeline & Notifications

Q7.1: When will I see event results?

A: Winners are announced instantly on the platform at event close (monetary amounts pending).

Q7.2: When do I receive payouts and SVX Points?

A: Within 24 hours post-event via a single consolidated email notification.

Security & Fairness

Q8.1: How do you prevent fraud?

A: We enforce server-side controls for all prize and points allocations, plus reCAPTCHA on sign-up and voting.

Q8.2: How are tied winners resolved?

A: First by earliest qualifying activity timestamp; if still tied, the prize is split equally.

Account & Data

Q9.1: Can I delete my account?

A: Yes—contact support to request data deletion, subject to pending payouts resolution.

Q9.2: Is my data safe?

A: We use encrypted storage, HTTPS, and strict access rules to protect your information.

Changes & Updates

Q10.1: How will I know about changes to these rules?

A: Material changes are announced via email and on the platform's dashboard.
