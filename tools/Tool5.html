Relative vs Absolute Units

Relative units (em, rem, %) scale based on parent or root elements. Absolute units (px, pt) are fixed sizes.

Best Practices

Use rem for consistent scaling, em for relative sizing within components, and px for precise control.

Responsive Typography

Combine viewport units with clamp() for fluid typography that scales smoothly across devices.
